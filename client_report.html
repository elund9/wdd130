General Experience and Reflections

The most challenging part of the this project was honestly working and communicating wiht my teamates. One of my groups was great, but it was a lot more difficult to communicate with my other groups.

I really enjoyed looking at other peoples ideas for a website, being able to contribute, and help make it.

If I could do this assignment differently, I would make sure to try more and different ways to communicate with my groups. I used email, Microsoft teams, and would talk in person, but I would exchange numbers to text and even call for better communicaion.

Yes, I feel more confident working on real world projects after this project. It gave me an idea of how it works to be a part of a team and make websites and webpages as a team.
